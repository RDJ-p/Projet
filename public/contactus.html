Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Just click on the Sign Up or Register button at the top of the page. Fill out your name, email, and password, then submit the form.

You’ll receive a confirmation email—follow the link to activate your account. Once you're in, you can start browsing, shopping, or participating in book exchanges!

How does the book exchange work?

Our book exchange program is a community-driven initiative where members can trade gently-used books with one another. After registering, you can list books you'd like to give away. When another member claims one, you earn a credit. You can then use that credit to request a book you want.

Each book exchanged is tracked via your account dashboard. You’ll receive shipping instructions via email. We encourage sending books within 3–5 business days to keep the experience smooth and engaging for everyone.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, and Apple Pay for online transactions. In-store payments can be made using cash, debit cards, or any contactless payment method.

All transactions are secured through encrypted gateways to ensure your data and purchases are protected at all times.

Can I return a purchased book?

Absolutely! We offer a 30-day return policy on all physical books that are in their original condition. Simply bring the book and your receipt to our store or contact customer support for mail-in returns.

E-books and digital purchases are non-refundable unless there’s a technical issue with your download. Reach out and we’ll help resolve it.

How do I participate in author events?

Visit our Events page to browse upcoming author signings, virtual Q&As, and exclusive readings. Each event listing will indicate whether registration is required.

For popular events, we recommend signing up early. Members may get priority access and occasional event discounts.

What condition should exchange books be in?

Books submitted for exchange should be in good condition. That means: no torn or missing pages, no excessive writing or highlighting, and clean covers/bindings.

We strive to maintain a high-quality collection, so damaged or heavily worn books will not be accepted. When in doubt, ask yourself if you'd enjoy receiving it!
